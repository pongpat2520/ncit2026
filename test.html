International Conference on Information Technology 2026

About nCIT 2026

The 10th International Conference on Information Technology (nCIT 2026) is a premier forum for sharing research in areas related to information and communication technologies. We believe that fostering research encompassing intelligent technology and innovation for the future of society is vital. Authors involved in these research areas are cordially invited to submit papers and present them at nCIT 2026. nCIT 2026 will be held in a hybrid format, offering both physical and online participation options. The physical conference will take place in Bangkok, Thailand, providing an excellent opportunity for researchers to meet face-to-face, strengthen collaborations, and engage in valuable academic discussions. For those unable to attend on-site, the online mode will ensure accessibility and enable remote participation from around the world. The conference will serve as an outstanding platform for participants to exchange and discuss innovative ideas and research findings, as well as explore future directions for cooperative research. It aims to foster collaboration among researchers and provide a stimulating environment for sharing knowledge and insights in the field of information technology.

We look forward to your participation in nCIT 2026, either physically in Bangkok or virtually through our online platform, as we work together to shape the future of intelligent technology and innovation for the benefit of society.

The conference provides an interdisciplinary platform for researchers to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the fields of Information Technology.


The 10th International Conference on Information Technology (nCIT 2026) is co-organized by IEEE Thailand Section (IEEE Computer Society Thailand Chapter), the Association of Council of IT Deans, and the Faculty of Information Technology,Siam University. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore, subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.
